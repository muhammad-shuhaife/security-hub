Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Cat6 and Cat6A?

+

Cat6 supports 1Gbps up to 100m and 10Gbps up to 55m. Cat6A supports 10Gbps across the full 100m with better alien crosstalk shielding ‚Äî ideal for high-density or data centre environments. We recommend the right choice based on your specific use case and future requirements.

Do you provide a warranty on the cabling installation?

+

Yes. We offer up to 25-year system warranties when using certified channel components. This covers both materials and performance guarantees backed by the manufacturer ‚Äî giving you long-term peace of mind on your infrastructure investment.

Will the installation disrupt our office operations?

+

We schedule work to minimise disruption ‚Äî typically undertaking major cable runs during off-hours or weekends. Our team works efficiently with clean methods to have zero impact on your day-to-day operations, and we provide a phased plan upfront so you know exactly what to expect.

Can you upgrade our existing cabling infrastructure?

+

Absolutely. We perform infrastructure audits, identify bottlenecks and substandard runs, and carry out targeted upgrades ‚Äî whether that's replacing old Cat5e with Cat6A, adding new data points, installing a fiber backbone between floors, or reorganising a messy rack.

When do I need fiber optic instead of copper cabling?

+

Fiber is recommended for backbone runs between floors or buildings (distances beyond 100m), high-speed connections between server rooms and switches, and environments with strong electrical interference. It provides near-unlimited bandwidth with zero electromagnetic susceptibility, making it the future-proof choice for any long-distance or high-performance link.
