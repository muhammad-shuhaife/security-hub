Frequently Asked Questions

Can the PA system integrate with our fire alarm panel?

+

Yes. We install EN54-compliant voice alarm systems that interface directly with fire alarm control panels, automatically triggering pre-recorded evacuation messages and overriding all other audio the moment a fire signal is received.

Can different zones play different audio simultaneously?

+

Absolutely. Our zone controller systems allow each zone to independently receive different audio sources ‚Äî the lobby can play background music while a floor receives a staff announcement, and the car park plays a different stream entirely, all simultaneously.

What type of speaker is best for a warehouse?

+

For high-noise, large-volume warehouse environments we recommend horn speakers ‚Äî they deliver high SPL output and are IP65-rated for dusty or wet conditions. We calculate the exact number and placement needed to achieve clear, intelligible audio throughout the entire space.

Can the system be expanded after installation?

+

Yes, all our PA systems are designed with future scalability in mind. Additional zones and speakers can be added without replacing the core equipment ‚Äî we plan spare capacity into the amplifier and controller specification from day one.

Does the PA system work during a power outage?

+

Yes. Our EN54-compliant emergency systems include dedicated battery backup to ensure the voice alarm and evacuation paging remain fully functional during a power cut ‚Äî a critical requirement for life-safety compliance in commercial buildings.
